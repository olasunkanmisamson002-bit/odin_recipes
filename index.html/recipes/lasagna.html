Lasagna

Description

Homemade lasagna is a classic Italian-inspired dish made with layers of tender pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella cheese. It is baked until golden and bubbly, creating a hearty and flavorful meal that is perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

This homemade lasagna recipe is easy to follow and delivers a delicious combination of savory beef, tomato sauce, herbs, and gooey cheese in every bite. Serve it with garlic bread and a fresh green salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened, then stir in the minced garlic.
  4. Add the ground beef and cook until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  5. Stir in the tomato sauce, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and black pepper. Simmer for 15–20 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, and half of the Parmesan cheese.
  7. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish. Add a layer of lasagna noodles, followed by the ricotta mixture, meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese.
  8. Repeat the layers until all the ingredients are used, finishing with mozzarella and the remaining Parmesan cheese on top.
  9. Bake for 35–45 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and golden brown.
  10. Let the lasagna rest for 10–15 minutes before slicing and serving.
