Pizza

Description

Homemade pizza is a delicious and customizable dish made with a soft, hand-prepared dough, rich tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and your favorite toppings. Whether you choose pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, bell peppers, or fresh basil, homemade pizza offers a fresh and flavorful alternative to takeout.

This recipe is simple to follow and perfect for family dinners, parties, or a fun weekend cooking activity. With a crispy golden crust, savory sauce, and gooey melted cheese, this homemade pizza is sure to become a favorite meal that everyone will enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 20 slices pepperoni (optional)
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup sliced onions
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Crushed red pepper flakes (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, yeast, salt, warm water, and olive oil. Mix until a dough forms, then knead for 8–10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  3. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it, and let it rise for about 1 hour, or until it has doubled in size.
  4. Punch down the dough and roll it out on a lightly floured surface into a 12-inch circle.
  5. Transfer the dough to a greased pizza pan or baking tray.
  6. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  7. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce, then add the pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, bell peppers, and any other desired toppings.
  8. Season the pizza with oregano, basil, and crushed red pepper flakes if desired.
  9. Bake the pizza for 12–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Remove the pizza from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve while hot.
